• Sample Survey for ISI M.Stat Entrance

    @ Cheenta School of Statistics and Data Science
    Course Duration : 6 weeks (February 2023 - April 2023)
    Class Timing : Tuesday 19:00 IST , Mode : Online
    Prerequisites : Undergraduate level Probability Theory

    About the coursework : Sample Survey is one of those topics which is more or less ignored / less popular among students at undergraduate level in India. Also, it is not a part of the IIT JAM syllabus but is included in ISI M.Stat entrance syllabus. This course attempts to make a student feel the essence of survey sampling in a probabilistic manner. We will cover the basics and only the basics of the topic, which will include a quick revision of discrete probability theory too. Hope you will enjoy this short course.

    Course Curriculum :

    1. Discrete Probability
    2. Simple Random Sampling (SRSWR/ SRSWOR)
    3. Stratified Random Sampling
    4. Cluster Sampling
    5. Two Stage Sampling
    6. Problem Solving : PYQ and miscellaneous

    Study Materials : Class notes will be provided after each class, which will contain exercise problems too. Also, the class whiteboard and recording will be shared to those who have joined the course. References for further interested students will be mentioned in the class notes only,

  • Probability Theory for IIT JAM MS and ISI M.Stat Entrance

    @ Cheenta School of Statistics and Data Science
    Course Duration : 18 weeks (May 2022 - September 2022)
    Class Timing : Monday 19:00 IST , Mode : Online
    Prerequisites : Elementary Real Analysis, Calculus and Linear Algebra

    About the coursework : Undoubtedly the most important topic in the foundational stage of a Statistics student. As you can see we have to cover the content of 4 semesters in 4 months, so the way of teaching won't be that much detailed in terms of doing derivations and calculations, we seek your effort to try those at home, it will also help you in competitive exams. As the studenst joining this course have already learnt probability at theor respective colleges, what I will try to do is give an essence of randomness in my own way, hopefully some perspectives of which you will find new and interesting. In a nutshell, the course will be more about learning for insight rather than learning formulas for solving problems.

    Course Curriculum :

    1. Classical Definition of Probability
    2. Axiomatic Definition of Probability
    3. Conditional Probability, Independence and Bayes' Theorem
    4. Random Variables, CDF, Generating Functions/li>
    5. Discrete and Continuous Random Variables
    6. Univariate and Bivariate Distributions
    7. Expectation, Variance, Moments, Correlation and Regression
    8. Standard Discrete Probability Distributions (Univariate)
    9. Standard Continuous Probability Distributions (Univariate)
    10. Probabilistic Inequalities
    11. Multivariate Probability Distributions
    12. Law of Large Numbers
    13. Modes of Convergence of Random Variables

  • Statistical Inference for IIT JAM MS

    @ Cheenta School of Statistics and Data Science
    Course Duration : 6 weeks (September 2021 - October 2021)
    Class Timing : Monday 19:00 IST , Mode : Online
    Prerequisites : Undergraduate level Probability Theory

    About the coursework : Actual learning of Statistics starts here, where mathematical foundations of probability Theory meets descriptive statistics. Very important for a budding statistician. My personal favourite course to teach. Again, less calculations and more insights due to short time span. Hope you find something new and helpful.

    Course Curriculum :

    1. Theory of Point Estimation
    2. Unbiasedness, Minimum Variance, Method of Moments
    3. MVUE, Cramer Rao Lower Bound
    4. Sufficiency, Completeness, Ancillarity
    5. Testing of Hypothesis, MP and UMP Tests, Confidence Intervals
    6. Maximum Likelihood Estimation
    7. Likelihood Ration Tests
